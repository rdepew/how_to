Opening the Network Connections window in Windows 10
====================================================

This is something that Windows 10 tries to hide. It's 
extremely useful and important at FreeWave.

Here's a webpage that tells, not just how to 
open the Network Connections window, but how to save
a shortcut to the Network Connections window on your
desktop.

https://winaero.com/blog/network-connections-shortcut-windows-10/

And in MobaXterm
================

It's easier in MobaXterm. Just type this at the prompt:

    open ncpa.cpl
